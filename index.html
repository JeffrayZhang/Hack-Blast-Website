FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?

All students and developers are welcome! No prior hackathon experience required.

How much does it cost?

Hack Blast is completely free! We'll provide meals, snacks, and all the energy drinks you need.

What should I bring?

Bring your laptop, charger, and enthusiasm! We'll provide everything else.

Do I need a team?

You can come solo and form a team at the event, or bring your own team of up to 4 people.

What can I build?

Anything! Web apps, mobile apps, hardware projects, games - if you can code it, you can build it.

Will there be mentors?

Yes! Industry professionals will be available throughout the event to help with technical challenges.
